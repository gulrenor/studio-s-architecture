Welcome To

Studio

Studio S Architecture is a firm dedicated to providing the highest level of design & service to our clients.

Whether it is a large corporate headquarters ground up project, or a small interior renovation, we strive to give every project the focus & attention required to achieve your goals. With over 20 years of experience in architectural design and construction management, Studio S Architecture is an innovative, collaborative, and experienced firm that will work with you to turn your ideas into reality.

Studio S Architecture is registered in the following states: MA, ME, NH, VT, RI, CT, NY, NJ, PA, WV, VA, MD, DE, and KY

  • Vincent James Sorrentino, AIA, NCARB

    Principal/Owner

    Received his Bachelor of Architecture degree from Roger Williams University in 2005. He studied art, architecture and architectural history in Italy, France and throughout Europe while attending the Institute of Liberal Arts at Palazzo Rucellai in Florence. Over the past 20 years he has been an integral part of countless design & construction projects from site design, ground up construction, to space planning and interior design.
